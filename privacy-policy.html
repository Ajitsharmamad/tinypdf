Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 27, 2025

Welcome to TinyPDF Tools (“we”, “our”, or “the website”). Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our tools.

1. No File Uploads — 100% Client-Side

TinyPDF processes all PDF, image, and document files directly in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server. All processing (compressing, converting, merging, splitting, rotating, etc.) happens locally on your device using client-side JavaScript libraries.

This means:

  • We do not store your files.
  • We do not view your files.
  • We do not transmit your files over the internet.
  • Your documents stay private and secure.

2. Information We DO Collect

We collect basic, non-personal technical data through third-party services to improve website performance.

a) Google Analytics

If Google Analytics is enabled, we may collect:

  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Device type, browser type
  • Approximate region (not exact location)
  • Time spent on pages

No personal information such as your name, email, IP address*, files, or documents is stored by us.

*IP anonymization is enabled, if configured.

b) Google AdSense (when enabled)

If ads are displayed, Google may use cookies to:

  • Show relevant ads
  • Limit repetitive ads
  • Measure ad performance

You can manage ad personalization at: https://www.google.com/settings/ads

3. Cookies

TinyPDF itself does not set any cookies.

However, third-party tools like Google Analytics or AdSense may set cookies for analytics or ad personalization.

4. External Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the content or policies of those websites.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
