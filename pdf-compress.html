Compress PDF — TinyPDF

Use the built-in Compress tool to quickly reduce PDF file size. This page explains when to compress, recommended targets, and step-by-step instructions.

How to compress a PDF (quick)

  1. Open the main TinyPDF Tools page: TinyPDF Tools and scroll to Compress PDF.
  2. Click Select PDF and choose your file.
  3. Click Compress and wait for the process to finish.
  4. Click Download to save the compressed PDF.

When to compress

Compress if you need to share PDFs via WhatsApp/email or reduce storage. Try 500KB–1MB targets for messaging apps.
